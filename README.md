## ESP Diffusion

Have you ever wanted to generate realisic digits on your smarthome IoT devices?

no? well too bad, now you can. By training a super small and simple diffusion
model that is able to fit in the 4mb of flash on the ESP32 I have delivered upon
that promise.

This was a fun way to learn the basics of embedded rust, and I'm going to work
on a more complex project in the next couple of weeks.
